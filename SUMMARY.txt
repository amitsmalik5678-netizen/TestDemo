App: Spring Boot + H2 + JWT + Swagger



Swagger:
  http://localhost:8080/swagger-ui.html

Auth:
  Get token: POST http://localhost:8080/auth/token
  Use header: Authorization: Bearer <token>

Users API:
  POST /users            (public)  body: {"name","city","companyName"}
  GET  /users            (JWT)
  GET  /users/{id}       (JWT)

DB (H2 Console):
  http://localhost:8080/h2-console
  JDBC: jdbc:h2:mem:testdb, user: sa, pass: (empty)
